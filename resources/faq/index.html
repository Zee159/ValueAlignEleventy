General Questions

What is ValueAlign?

ValueAlign is a digital platform designed to help individuals and organizations understand, clarify, and align with their core values. We provide scientifically validated assessments, guided exercises, and reflection tools that help you discover what matters most to you and how to live more authentically in alignment with those values.

How does ValueAlign work?

Our platform works through several integrated components:

  • Values Assessment: A scientifically designed questionnaire that helps identify your core personal values
  • Guided Exercises: Structured activities to explore how your values apply to different areas of life
  • Reflection Journal: A personal space to record insights and track your journey
  • Goal Setting: Tools to set objectives aligned with your values and monitor your progress
  • Resources: Educational materials to deepen your understanding of values-based living

Who can benefit from using ValueAlign?

ValueAlign is designed for a wide range of users, including:

  • Individuals seeking greater clarity, purpose, and authenticity in life
  • Teams working to improve collaboration through shared understanding of values
  • Organizations aiming to build stronger, values-aligned cultures
  • Coaches and Therapists supporting clients in personal development
  • Educators teaching character development and ethical decision-making

Our platform is accessible to anyone interested in personal growth and living more intentionally.

Is there scientific research behind ValueAlign?

Yes. ValueAlign's assessment and methodology are grounded in decades of psychological research on values, well-being, and behavior change. Our approach draws from established frameworks in positive psychology, acceptance and commitment therapy, and values theory. The assessment has been validated through multiple studies and is regularly updated to incorporate new findings in values research.

For more information about our research foundations, visit our Research Page.

How is ValueAlign different from other personality tests?

Unlike personality tests that categorize who you are, ValueAlign focuses on what matters to you. Key differences include:

  • Focus on Values: We explore what's meaningful to you rather than personality traits
  • Action-Oriented: Our platform helps you apply insights through practical exercises
  • Growth Perspective: Values can be clarified and strengthened, not just identified
  • Holistic Approach: We integrate assessment, reflection, and application
  • Scientific Foundation: Based on established research in values psychology

Values Assessment

What is the Values Assessment?

The ValueAlign Assessment is a comprehensive questionnaire designed to help you identify and prioritize your core values. It measures how important different values are to you across multiple life domains and provides insights into potential value conflicts or areas for growth.

The assessment takes approximately 15-20 minutes to complete and results in a detailed personal values profile with actionable insights.

How often should I take the Values Assessment?

We recommend taking the full assessment:

  • When you first join ValueAlign
  • After significant life changes or transitions
  • Annually, as part of ongoing self-reflection

While core values tend to be relatively stable, your understanding of them and their priority in your life may evolve over time. Regular reassessment helps you track this evolution and ensure your goals and actions remain aligned with your current values.

Can I retake just parts of the assessment?

Yes, ValueAlign offers focused mini-assessments for specific life domains (work, relationships, personal growth, etc.) that take 3-5 minutes each. These are useful for:

  • Exploring how your values apply in specific contexts
  • Preparing for important decisions in particular areas
  • Checking in on areas where you're experiencing challenges

Premium users can access these mini-assessments anytime from their dashboard.

How are my assessment results stored and protected?

Your assessment results are:

  • Stored securely in your encrypted personal account
  • Never shared with third parties without your explicit consent
  • Protected by our comprehensive data security protocols
  • Available for you to download or delete at any time

For more detailed information, please see our Privacy Policy.

What if I disagree with my assessment results?

The assessment is designed to be a starting point for exploration, not a definitive statement about your values. If some results don't feel right:

  • Reflect on why certain results feel misaligned
  • Use the guided reflection exercises to explore further
  • Consider whether there might be a gap between your aspirational values (what you wish you valued) and your actual values (what drives your decisions)
  • Remember that the assessment can only capture what you report, and self-awareness takes time to develop

You can always retake the assessment after some reflection, or use the results as a starting point for deeper exploration through journaling and exercises.

Account & Billing

What subscription plans does ValueAlign offer?

ValueAlign offers several subscription options to meet different needs:

  • Free Basic: Includes the initial values assessment and limited access to exercises and resources
  • Individual Premium: Full access to all assessments, exercises, journal features, and resources
  • Family Plan: Premium features for up to 5 family members at a discounted rate
  • Teams: Specialized features for workplace teams, including collaborative exercises and team insights
  • Enterprise: Custom solutions for large organizations with advanced analytics and integration options

Visit our Pricing Page for detailed information about features and current rates.

How do I upgrade or change my subscription plan?

You can manage your subscription at any time by following these steps:

  1. Log in to your ValueAlign account
  2. Navigate to Account Settings in the dashboard menu
  3. Select the Subscription tab
  4. Click Change Plan to view available options
  5. Select your desired plan and follow the prompts to complete the change

Plan changes typically take effect immediately for upgrades or at the end of your current billing period for downgrades.

How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your ValueAlign subscription:

  1. Log in to your ValueAlign account
  2. Navigate to Account Settings in the dashboard menu
  3. Select the Subscription tab
  4. Click Cancel Subscription
  5. Follow the prompts to confirm cancellation

After cancellation, you'll continue to have access to premium features until the end of your current billing period. Your account will then revert to the Free Basic level, and you'll retain access to your assessment history and journal entries.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, ValueAlign offers a 30-day satisfaction guarantee for new subscribers. If you're not satisfied with your premium subscription within the first 30 days, you can request a full refund by contacting our support team at support@valuealign.com.

For subscription periods beyond the initial 30 days, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. Please contact our support team with your specific situation, and we'll work with you to find an appropriate solution.

What payment methods do you accept?

ValueAlign accepts the following payment methods:

  • Credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay (for iOS app subscriptions)
  • Google Pay (for Android app subscriptions)

For enterprise accounts, we also accept purchase orders and direct bank transfers. Please contact our sales team at sales@valuealign.com for more information.

Privacy & Security

How does ValueAlign protect my personal data?

ValueAlign employs multiple layers of security to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols
  • Secure Authentication: We use multi-factor authentication options and secure password requirements
  • Regular Security Audits: Our systems undergo regular security assessments and penetration testing
  • Data Minimization: We only collect information necessary for providing our services
  • Access Controls: Strict internal controls limit who can access user data and under what circumstances

For comprehensive details about our security practices, please review our Privacy Policy.

Who can see my assessment results and journal entries?

Your privacy is paramount to us. By default, all your assessment results, journal entries, and personal reflections are private and visible only to you.

For specific situations, you have the option to share certain information:

  • Team Assessments: If you participate in a team assessment, aggregate (anonymized) team data may be shared with team leaders
  • Coaching Integration: You can choose to share specific results with coaches or therapists you're working with
  • Research Participation: You may opt in to contribute anonymized data to ongoing values research

Any sharing requires your explicit consent, which you can withdraw at any time through your privacy settings.

Does ValueAlign use cookies or tracking technologies?

Yes, ValueAlign uses the following types of cookies and similar technologies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality and security
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our platform to improve the experience

You can manage your cookie preferences through our Cookie Consent Manager, accessible via the link in the website footer. For complete information about our cookie usage, please see our Cookie Policy.

How do I delete my account and all my data?

To delete your account and associated data:

  1. Log in to your ValueAlign account
  2. Navigate to Account Settings in the dashboard menu
  3. Select the Privacy tab
  4. Click on Delete Account at the bottom of the page
  5. Follow the verification steps to confirm account deletion

When you delete your account:

  • All your personal information will be permanently removed from our active systems
  • Your assessment results, journal entries, and other content will be deleted
  • Any anonymized data you've contributed to research (if you opted in) will remain anonymized

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. If you have any difficulties, please contact our support team at privacy@valuealign.com.

What are my data rights as a user?

As a ValueAlign user, you have several rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can download a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing

To exercise any of these rights, visit the Privacy section in your Account Settings or contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@valuealign.com.

For more detailed information about your data rights and how to exercise them, please see our Privacy Policy.
