About ValueAlign

Our mission is to help you create a life of meaning through values-based action.

Our Mission

ValueAlign was created to bridge the gap between knowing what matters to you and actually living that way daily. In a world full of distractions and autopilot behaviors, we help you pause, reflect, and take actions that align with your deepest values.

Founded by a team of psychologists, technologists, and mindfulness practitioners, we combine behavioral science with practical tools that fit into your daily life.

The Science

ValueAlign draws from multiple evidence-based approaches including Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), positive psychology, behavioral economics, and mindfulness practices.

Our methodology has been developed alongside researchers and practitioners to ensure an approach that is both scientifically sound and practically useful in everyday life.
